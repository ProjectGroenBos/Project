Our Airpoints programme is all about getting you where you want to go quicker. You can earn Airpoints DollarsTM in the air or on the ground and spend them on flights, exclusive seat upgrades and even some fine wine.
Here are a couple of ways you can keep track of your Airpoints DollarsTM
Download the Air NZ mobile app
Download the Air NZ mobile app to book flights, check in and manage your travel plans. Plus access your digital card and keep a record of your Airpoints number, Airpoints Dollar balance and Status Points on the go.
Set up an online account
You can set up an Airpoints account online at airnz.com for a quick and easy way to keep track of your Airpoints Dollars and Status Points.
